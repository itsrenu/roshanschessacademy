Meet Roshan

Indiana State Champion with a Mission to Transform Young Lives Through Chess

More Than Just a Chess Champion

As an Indiana State Chess Champion, I discovered something powerful: chess isn't just a game—it's a life teacher. Every move on the board mirrors decisions we make in life. Every loss teaches resilience. Every victory builds confidence.

My Vision

"I believe every child has the potential to think strategically, overcome challenges, and develop unshakeable confidence. Through chess, I'm not just teaching moves—I'm nurturing future leaders who can face any obstacle with grace and determination."

Passionate Educator

Character Builder

Authentic Mentor

Creating a disruptive environment where every child feels valued and encouraged to grow

Why I Do This

Having experienced firsthand how chess shaped my character and strategic thinking, I'm driven by a simple belief: every child deserves the confidence and life skills that chess provides. My mission is to make these invaluable lessons accessible, enjoyable, and transformative for the next generation.

When I see a shy child make their first bold move, or watch a frustrated student learn to bounce back from defeat, I know I'm not just teaching chess—I'm helping shape resilient, thoughtful leaders of tomorrow.
